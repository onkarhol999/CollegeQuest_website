Arunodaya University,

History

Arunachal University of Studies (AUS) is a private university located at Namsai.The Arunachal University of Studies (AUS) has been established by Govt. of Arunachal Pradesh through an Act of State Legislature as the State University under the sponsorship and management of World Education Mission (WEM). The university is duly recognized by UGC u/s 2(f) and is empowered to award degrees specified u/s 22 of UGC Act 1956. AUS is the member of Association of Indian Universities (AIU), New Delhi. The campus is situated at the serene location of Namsai district in Arunachal Pradesh, which is situated at a strategic location within in the proximity of 12 Districts Eastern Arunachal Pradesh, 16 Districts of Upper Assam and 4 Districts of Nagaland. The entire region is dominated by tribal population and most of the students of AUS belonged to the minority and tribal groups. Being one of the very few higher education The university has entered into Khadi & Village Industry Commission (KVIC); Indian Institute of Entrepreneurship (IIE); National Institute for Entrepreneurship & Small Business Development (NIESBUD); National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC); National Council for Sports & Youth Development (NCSYD); Aalborg University; Mejo University; Chiangmai University; Northumbria University and Coventry University. The university is also undertaking the projects recommended by various Govt. organizations which includes Ministry of Earth Sciences; Rehabilitation Council of India; Department of Science & Technology; Ministry of Rural Development; Ministry of Minority Affairs; Indian Council for Agriculture Research; ISRO, Department of Space, Govt. of India; GSDP, Ministry of Environment and Climate Change; etc.[citation needed] The AUS is offering Diploma, Graduation, Post-Graduation and Doctoral level Programme in the field of Pharmacy, Nursing, Paramedical, Agricultural Sciences, Science, Engineering & Technology, Library & Information Sciences, Education, Special Education, Physical Education, Arts, Social Sciences, Design, Performing Arts, Commerce, Management and Legal Studies.

The picturesque and surrounding of Jote makes it ideal for a noble academic environment.[6] Facilities The facilities and services that NITAP renders to the students, faculty members and staff make it stand among the top level institutes. The amenities provided to the students make the stay in NITAP a good experience. The presence of basic facilities such as 24 X 7 availability water and electricity and a variety of food for different teste. The institute and hostels are equipped with Wifi and LAN facility so as to get relevant information for academic development. NIT Arunachal Pradesh is a fully residential campus. There are five hostels for boys, namely Deer Hall of Residence, Mithun Hall of Residence, Lagoon Hall of Residence, Pragati Hall of Residence, and Tiger Hall of Residence and three hostels for girls, namely Rose Hall of Residence, Hornbill Hall of Residence and Peacock Hall of Residence. The mess is run privately. Each hostel is under the care of a warden. Sufficient number of buses have been provided by the institute and the state government for the students to travel from their hostels to the institute.
